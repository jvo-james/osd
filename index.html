Urban Elegant • Cozy Home

An intentional evening, crafted inside a 1954 home.

Oaklawn Southern Dining offers chef-inspired dishes in a unique urban-elegant atmosphere—minutes from Paris Landing State Park and Land Between the Lakes. You will love the view from the old Oaklawn home built in 1954, remade with dining and hospitality in mind.

Prime Steaks

Hand-selected cuts, cooked to order, finished with elegant sauces and classic sides.

Prime Seafood

Market-driven seafood, prepared with bold Southern technique and refined presentation.

Bourbon & Paris-Inspired Cocktails

A deep bourbon list, signature cocktails, and a curated wine & champagne selection.
