The Experience

Intentional dining. Thoughtful hospitality.

Oaklawn Southern Dining offers chef-inspired dishes in a unique urban elegant atmosphere. In a remodeled 1954 home, every detail is designed to elevate the full evening—date night, celebration, or a crafted cocktail to take the edge off a long day.

A home reimagined

The old Oaklawn home (built in 1954) offers a view and atmosphere designed for comfort, elegance, and unforgettable evenings.

Prime cuts, crafted

Well-sought-after quality steaks, cooked to order, paired with sauces and glazes that complement—not compete.

Prime seafood

Bourbon & Paris-inspired cocktails

About

Southern dining, elevated.

Oaklawn Southern Dining offers chef-inspired dishes in a unique urban elegant atmosphere. Enjoy the view from the old Oaklawn home built in 1954—remade with dining and hospitality in mind.

Near Paris Landing State Park
Minutes from Land Between the Lakes

Our menu brings flavors from across the nation, featuring well sought-after quality steaks and prime seafood. We’re intentional about your whole evening experience—from a girl’s night out to an anniversary, or simply helping you take the edge off a long day.

Choose from Paris-inspired crafted cocktails, one of our many bourbons, and an excellent wine and champagne list to complement your meal or special evening.
